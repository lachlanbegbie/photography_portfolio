I'm Hugh, a photographer based in Sydney, specializing in landscapes and urban scenes. I focus on capturing natural light and the unique features of the environments I shoot, whether it's the coastline or the city streets. My style is straightforward, highlighting clean lines and strong contrasts without overcomplicating the shot. I aim to create images that feel honest and grounded.
